Miltonious

This album was recording over a period of several years. Most tracks are remastered 4 track recordings of vocal and guitar performed live by Hank Milton DeMeerleer (originally written by Hank throughout the 1970s).

“My Time With You”, “Lonesome Planet” and “Into Reality (Remix)” contain musical performances and editing by Zebulon Pike DeMeerleer.

All Tracks Written by Hank Milton DeMeerleer

Produced by Zebulon Pike DeMeerleer


All music also available on all major music streaming services...
