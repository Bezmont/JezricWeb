Other Sides


Three years and so much change. With mixed emotions about the state of the world and humanity within, I question, is it really all that different? Through isolation and thoughts of an uncertain future these fourteen songs have come to be. Crafted of keys, wires, reeds, sticks, voices, electricity and imagination these songs are the next offering to all those willing to listen.

Much of the sounds on this album come from the beautifully crafted circuitry of synthesizers with names like the Arturia PolyBrute, Sequential Pro-3, Prophet Rev2 and the Korg Modwave.


Mixed with a variety of electric and acoustic instruments including saxophone from Keith Malsbary and the lovely vocals of Erica DeMeerleer, a rich collection of sounds is presented for your pleasure. If you are willing, listen closely; there are a lot of details on the other sides.
