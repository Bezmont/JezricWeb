Turners Corner was a three-piece band based in Seattle Washington, USA. Described as rock and power pop, Turners Corner creates music deriving from such influences as The Cure, Gary Numan and Pavement, as well as Pink Floyd, Rush, Steely Dan, and The Police. The members of Turners Corner grew up in Woodinville WA. Singer/Guitar player Todd Maloy and Bass/Synth player Zebulon DeMeerleer played in the band Stella Grace in the early to mid 90's. Several years later they regrouped with a new drummer Brian DeMeerleer.

Having produced one self titled album, Turners Corner played shows around Seattle throughout 2004 - 2006.
